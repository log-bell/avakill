Last updated: February 27, 2026

The short version
  • We collect your email only if you subscribe to updates
  • No analytics, no tracking scripts, no cookies
  • The AvaKill software itself collects nothing
  • Unsubscribe anytime with one click

•What we collect

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. That's it. We don't collect names, IP addresses, browser fingerprints, or any other personal data.

The AvaKill open-source software does not collect, transmit, or phone home any data. Everything runs locally on your machine.

•Why we collect it

We use your email to send product updates: new releases, new integrations, and security advisories. We don't sell, share, or rent your email to anyone.

•Who processes it

Email subscriptions are processed by Loops, our email platform. Loops stores your email address on their servers to deliver messages on our behalf. See the Loops privacy policy for details on their data handling.

•Cookies and tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics scripts, or tracking pixels. We don't know how many people visit this site, and that's fine.

•How to opt out

Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. Click it and you're removed immediately. You can also email us and we'll remove you manually.

Questions? Open an issue on GitHub or reach out on Discord.
